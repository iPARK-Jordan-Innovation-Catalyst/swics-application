Scaling Up Water Innovation for Climate Security in Northern Jordan

Online Application

Scaling Up Water Innovation for Climate Security in Northern Jordan Program is funded by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and implemented by iPARK and the Royal Scientific Society (RSS).

Program overview

This program aims to enhance climate security among Syrian refugees and host communities by supporting local water innovations, entrepreneurs and SMEs to pilot and scale-up their innovative solutions among local communities, thus building an expandable model that can be replicated and scaled up for longer-term sustainable development.
